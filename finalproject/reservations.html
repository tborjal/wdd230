Vehicle Rental Agreement for Cozumel, Mexico

- This Vehicle Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the Renter, as identified in the booking reservation, and the Company, a vehicle rental service operating in Cozumel, Mexico. - The Company is committed to providing a well-maintained and roadworthy vehicle for your convenience and enjoyment during your stay in Cozumel, Mexico. Our vehicles are regularly inspected and serviced to meet safety standards and ensure reliable performance on the roads.

- As the Renter, it is essential that you provide accurate and complete information regarding your driver's license, contact details, and any other requested information. This enables us to complete the necessary paperwork and documentation required for the rental, and helps us provide you with the best possible service.

- During the rental period, you are responsible for the condition and safe operation of the rental vehicle. Any damages or loss incurred during the rental period will be the responsibility of the Renter, subject to the terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement.

Insurance

- The rental vehicle comes with a basic liability insurance that covers damage to the third party. The Renter can choose to upgrade the insurance to include additional coverages that may include, but not limited to: collision coverage, theft protection, personal accident insurance, and additional claims administration fees.

- When renting a vehicle in Cozumel, Mexico, it is crucial to understand the insurance options available. The basic liability insurance, included in the rental price, provides coverage for damages to third-party property or persons. For additional protection, the Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) reduces the Renter's financial responsibility for any damage or loss to the rental vehicle, with a deductible amount specified in the rental agreement.

- Theft protection coverage reduces financial responsibility in case of theft or attempted theft, while personal accident insurance provides medical expenses and personal accident benefits in case of injury or death resulting from an accident while in the rental vehicle. Optional coverage selections and corresponding costs will be outlined in the rental agreement.

- It is essential to carefully review the terms and conditions of each insurance option to make an informed decision that meets your needs and provides peace of mind during your rental period.
