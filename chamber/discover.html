Our Beloved Riverbanks!

Marikina City, known as the Shoe Capital of the Philippines, is not only famous for its footwear industry but also boasts scenic riverbanks that attract locals and tourists alike. In this content, we will explore the charm of Marikina City's riverbanks, highlighting its natural beauty, recreational activities, and vibrant atmosphere.

  • Lush greenery and well-maintained landscapes along the riverbanks. Beautiful views of the flowing river and surrounding nature. Notable landmarks and attractions, such as parks, gardens, and bridges.
  • Jogging and biking paths that provide opportunities for exercise and leisure, Picnic spots and open spaces for gatherings and relaxation, Amenities like exercise areas and bike lanes for fitness enthusiasts.
  • Historical and cultural importance tied to the city's heritage, Art installations, sculptures, and architectural features that enhance the cultural ambiance, Significance of the riverbanks in local events and traditions.
  • Local restaurants, cafes, and food stalls offering a range of cuisines, Entertainment venues and events, including live music performances and festivals, Vibrant atmosphere for socializing and enjoying leisure time.
  • Active involvement of the community in maintaining the cleanliness and beauty of the riverbanks. Community initiatives promoting environmental awareness and sustainability. Opportunities for community participation and interaction.

The Amazing Marikina Sports Center

The Sports Center in Marikina City is a premier sports complex that offers state-of-the-art facilities for athletes and fitness enthusiasts alike. This modern facility offers a wide range of amenities and activities that cater to people of all ages and skill levels.

The Sports Center highlights include its Olympic-sized swimming pool, indoor and outdoor basketball courts, tennis courts, a football field, a fitness center, an obstacle course, a skate park, and a children's playground. It also offers sports clinics, tournaments, and events for locals and tourists throughout the year. Additionally, The Sports Center has ample parking, free Wi-Fi, and a food court serving healthy meals and snacks to fuel your activity.

The Shoe Capital of the Philippines

Marikina City, located in the Philippines, is known as the "Shoe Capital of the Philippines" because of its prominent shoe-making industry. In commemoration of this title, a giant pair of shoes was constructed, measuring 5.5 meters long, 2.25 meters wide, and 1.83 meters high. This creation is made of genuine leather, as per city ordinance. In 2002, it was recognized by the Guinness World Records as the largest shoes in the world. The giant shoes of Marikina have since been considered a city attraction, attracting international tourism and promoting the advancement of this industry in the country.
